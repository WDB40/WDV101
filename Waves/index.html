Welcome to WAVES

Ever thought of picking up a surf board and paddling out to ocean instead of just laying in the sun? Well, think about it no more. Take the first steps! WAVES offers a wide variety of options for you to start your journey.

Lessons

If you're looking to just dip your toes in and figure out how to stand up on the boards then our Private Lessons will be perfect for you.

If you're wanting to go a step further and earn that beach sticker on the back of your car then our Private Packages are right up your alley. They come in a variety of sizes from three lessons to seven.

Well, what if you're just looking to have a little fun with your friends while learning a little bit about surfing at the same time? Then our beginner Group Lessons are what you are looking for!

But what if you already have some experience surfing and you're wanting to learn from the instructors and other surfers? Well, may we re-introduce you to our Group Lessons, but this time they are our Master Level lessons.

Camps

Lastly, if you're looking to get the most out of the warm weather while we have it, check out our Camps. They provide you an intense experience that tries to jam as much surfing knowledge into you as quickly as possible (and hopefully it lasts)!

If you have any questions, please contact us!
