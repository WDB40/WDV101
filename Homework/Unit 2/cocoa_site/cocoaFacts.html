Many Different Names

The cocoa bean is known through a similar name cacao. Pronounced like ka-cow, it comes from the name Theobroma cacao.

Used in Drinks Other than Coffee

The cocoa bean is used along with maize (a.k.a. corn) to create the drink tejate.

A Wide Variety - Or Is There?

While today, you can shop at any local supermarket and find dozens of different chocolate bars, 80-90% of the world production of cocoa plants from three main varieties of the plant. They are Forastero, Criollo, and Trinitario.

Bean Rich Chocolate

To create a single kilogram of chocolate (which is about 2.2 pounds), it can take anywhere between 300 to 600 to complete the process.
