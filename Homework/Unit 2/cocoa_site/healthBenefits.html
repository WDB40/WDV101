Many see chocolate in the same group as all the other treats in the candy isle. But is that all true...?

Chocolate is Packed with Nutritious Value

Consuming a 100-gram bar of that sweet, dark chocolate will net you:

  • 11 grams of fiber
  • 67% of iron
  • 58% of magnesium
  • 89% of copper
  • 98 of manganese

The percentages tie to the recommended daily intake.

Improves Your Flow

Your blood flow that is. With the rising concerns around blood pressure, chocolate may be the answer for a few people. In many studies, chocolate has been shown to improve blood flow and lower blood pressure.

Won't Let You Melt

Just like a nice chocolate bar, you will melt if left in the sun. Well, maybe not melt, but you can cause serious damage to your skin if you stay out too long. The flavonols in chocolate protects, hydrates, and improves blood flow for the skin.
